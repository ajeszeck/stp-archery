STRAIGHT TO THE POINT ARCHERY

Welcome!

Straight to the Point Archery is an indoor training facility
with 28 shooting (14 ten-yard lanes and 14 twenty-yard lanes)
and two private coaching rooms.
We also have access to 26 additional twenty-yard lanes 
and an outdoor shooting field.

"We are dedicated to teaching valuable life skills to the youth in our community through the wonderful discipline of archery."
—Giap Nguyen

Owner and founder of Straight to the Point Archery
